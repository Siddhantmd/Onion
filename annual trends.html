    Times of dramatic rise in prices:
  1. Prices rose in 1997 end and began falling in 1998. In 1998 prices rose steeply until October, peaking at ₹3,000
  2. In 2005, prices rose upto three fold, peaking in october at around ₹1,300
  3. In 2007, there was a minor peak in prices in February. Prices again peaked majorly in October.
  4. In 2008, prices increased steadily in the latter half of the year. They peaked in January 2009 and fell until May.
    May onwards, prices rose steadily till September, when the rise caught pace and stabilised at a higher point for the next two months
  5. The year 2010 saw an exponential rise May onwards. The prices rose till January 2011 and fell steeply until March.
  6. 2013 saw dramatic rise in prices, peaking in September-October at around ₹4,800
  7. In 2014, prices peaked in July at around ₹2,400
  8. 2015 again saw a dramatic rise in prices which peaked in September at around ₹4,600
